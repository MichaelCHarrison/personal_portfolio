Flex Flex FLEX

September 20, 2016 by Michael

Flex Flex FLEX

I first heard the boys from Belfast whilst on a boat - specifially, a track of their's called "Just" played by Pedro Winter in the discotheque of the MSC Divina. It totally blew me away. A sonic experience which left an impression that would linger for not days, but for MONTHS after. Despite this, it would be some time until I really dove in to discover what the Irish duo had to offer.

Childhood friends turned bloggers turned musicians turned underground dance music superstars. With appearances on everything from rinse.fm to esteemed guest spots on BBC radio1, the ever flexing pair has made a name through playing some of the most sonically interesting dance music on the planet. And to add, a production aesthetic that is undeniably Bicep, featuring tight percussive programming balanced with soulful vocal sampling that comes together in perfect, dance floor driving harmony.

About

I've been a life long musician always searching for the next means by which to express myself and my musical thought - this is what's next.

Musician by classical training, DJ by passion, dance floor slayer by fate - I live for the next surface to dance upon to connect with the sounds, the space, and the souls that inhabit that surface til sun up.
